Our Kitties

Thinking about adopting a cat? Come stop by and meet all of our feline friends. Every kitty deserves to live a happy, comfortable life in a loving home.
We encourage you and your family to spend a good amount of time with each cat to see if they are the best fit. If you or your family have any questions, our staff on board will happily be ready to answer!

Our mission is to find the perfect connection between you and the cats. With cats, they need to be able to trust you and feel at home with you.

What To Consider Before Adopting

  • All pets are a major commitment.

  • A pet's lifespan is typically around 12-18 years.

  • Pets are a financial commitment. From food, vet visits, pet insurance, medications, etc.
